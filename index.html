Who am I?

David Palos

3rd Year Art Major

Attending University of California Irvine:
Claire Trevor School of the Arts

My name is David Palos and I am a third-year art student at the Claire Trevor School of the Arts
department at the University of California Irvine. I am originally from Bellflower, California,
United States. My central goal is and always has been to find a career in the digital production
and post-production fields of filmmaking. This includes a variety of fields such as editing, but I
have a particular interest in visual effects artistry. This means that although I may not be too
experienced with it, I have always had a strong interest in technology and the art that we can create
with digital mediums. This has been the case since I was a child and was largely influenced by the
original Star Wars trilogy, which pushed the boundaries of filmmaking and found unique ways to make
their vision a reality despite having various limitations with the technology of the time. Although I
still have a long way to go before I can begin to indulge in this kind of work, I aim to improve day by
day and I am always excited to find a new opportunity to learn about two topics that I have always been
passionate about, technology and art!
